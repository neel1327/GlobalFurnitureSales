**Summary**

This dashboard provides a holistic view of global furniture sales performance, combining key metrics, trend analysis, and segmentation insights. It serves as a powerful tool for business stakeholders to monitor growth, optimize sales strategies, and enhance overall operational efficiency.

**Key Insights & Analysis**

1.  Time-Based Performance Analysis
Revenue shows a significant increase from 2021 to 2022, indicating business growth
Monthly margin trends highlight seasonal fluctuations and peak sales periods
Helps identify high-performing months and demand cycles
2.  Regional Performance Analysis
Sales are distributed across:
North East
South West
North West
Certain regions contribute a higher percentage of total customers and revenue, indicating strong market presence
Enables region-wise strategy optimization
3.  Product Performance Analysis
Top-performing products include:
Bamboo Furniture
Marble Dining Tables
Sofa Sets
The dashboard compares:
Revenue vs Product Count
Helps identify:
High-revenue, low-volume products (premium segment)
High-volume, moderate-revenue products
4.  Customer Segmentation Analysis
Key customer segments:
Style-focused customers
Home improvement segment
Metro/urban customers
Analysis includes:
Number of items purchased per customer type
Salesperson contribution per customer
Enables targeted marketing and customer-focused strategies
5.  Salesperson Performance
Distribution of sales across salespersons
Identifies:
Top-performing sales representatives
Customer handling efficiency
Useful for performance evaluation and incentive planning

**Features & Functionality**

Interactive Filters (Slicers) for customer-level analysis
Drill-down Capabilities for deeper insights into product and regional data
Dynamic Visualizations including:
KPI Cards
Line Charts (trend analysis)
Bar Charts (product comparison)
Donut Charts (distribution analysis)
Cross-filtering across all visuals for real-time exploration

**Data Processing & Modeling**

Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
Built data models and relationships for structured analysis
Created DAX measures for:
Revenue calculation
Margin analysis
Year-wise comparison
Optimized dataset for performance and usability

**Business Impact**

Enabled real-time tracking of sales KPIs
Identified top-performing regions and products driving revenue
Highlighted underperforming segments for improvement
Supported data-driven decision-making and strategic planning
Improved sales efficiency and revenue optimization
